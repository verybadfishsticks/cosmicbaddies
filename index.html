privacy policy

information we collect

cosmic is designed with privacy in mind. we do not collect personal information. all data is stored locally in your browser:

  • game preferences and favorites
  • settings and customizations
  • game play history (stored locally)

how we use information

the locally stored information is used to:

  • remember your favorite games
  • maintain your preferred settings
  • provide a personalized experience

data storage

all data is stored locally in your browser using localstorage. no data is transmitted to external servers except when:

  • loading games from external sources

  • accessing third-party tools and services
